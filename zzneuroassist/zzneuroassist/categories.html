CATEGORIES

ADHD

Affecting focus, impulse control, and activity levels. With the right support, individuals can harness their creativity, energy, and problem-solving skills.

ASD

Condition affecting communication, social interaction, and behavior. Each individual has unique strengths, and support fosters growth and inclusion.

Dyslexia

Learning difference that affects reading, writing, and spelling but not intelligence. With the right support, individuals can excel using their creativity and problem-solving skills.
